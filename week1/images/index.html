Takoyaki

A must have to try Japanese street food!

Takoyaki is a ball-shaped Japanese snack made of a wheat flour-based batter and cooked in a special molded pan.

It is usually filled with minced or diced octopus, tempura scraps, pickled ginger, and green onion.

It is often served with takoyaki sauce, mayonnaise, and dried bonito flakes.

It is a popular street food in Japan and is also found in many izakaya and restaurants.
